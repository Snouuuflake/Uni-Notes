\begin{defbox}{3.1.14}{(Subsets)}
  Let A,B be sets. \(A \subseteq B \iff \forall x \in A, x \in B.\)

  \(A \subsetneq B \iff A \subseteq B \land A \neq B.\)
\end{defbox}
